ADDICTION

is a compulsive, chronic, physiological or psychological need for a habit-forming substance, behavior, or activity having harmful physical, psychological, or social effects and typically causing well-defined symptoms (such as anxiety, irritability, tremors, or nausea) upon withdrawal or abstinence. Typically there are different types of addictions: alcohol addiction, an addiction to prescription painkillers, drug addictions, gambling addiction.

YOU DON'T HAVE TO STRUGGLE IN SILENCE!

There are several methods of treatment we use at WEvolve such as evidence-based treatments: detoxification, cognitive behavioral therapy (which helps you recognize your unhealthy behavioral patterns and can also help you learn to identify triggers and develop coping skills), Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy (that helps you realize that the power of rational thinking lies within yourself and is not related to external situations or stressors), treatment with medications.

Here at WEvolve we practice holistic treatment in complex with evidence-based classical treatment, which helps curb withdrawal symptoms, serves as coping tools to prevent relapse, helps with mental health and emotional issues, and enhance mental, physical and spiritual wellbeing as well as overall quality of life.
