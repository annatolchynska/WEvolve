Are you at the point where you know your life has to change? Are you searching for more peace, fulfillment, and a sense of purpose?
At the WEvolve we understand the intricacy of the human mind.

WEvolve is a modern treatment centre and a mental health clinic, unlike traditional rehab centres. It was created (in 2007) to be a safe heaven where you can find recovery, peace, rest and happiness. Our qualified team of physicians and psychiatrists as well as our treatment facilities are here to provide you with an exceptional experience. Our centre offers you more than just treatment, we offer the guidance to rediscover your soul and achieve your best self.

Our specialty is a wide range of emotional, physical, and psychological issues covering all kinds of mental health issues. We specialize in treating Mental health disorders, such as: Bipolar disorder, Dysthymia, Trauma, Anxiety, Panic attacks, Chronic pain syndrome, Emotional crisis, Mood disorders & depression, Sleeping disorders, Isolation & emptiness, Selfharm, Suicidal thoughts, Relation & communication, Chronic fatique, Severe anxiety; Eating disorders; Burnout; Stress disorders, Personality disorder and other.
